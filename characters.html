Characters

Jerry Seinfeld is the main character on the TV show Seinfeld. He lives in New York and hangs out with his friends George, Elaine, and Kramer. Together, they live life and end up in comical situations.

Elaine Benes is one of Jerry's close friends and ex-girlfriend. She's intelligent, funny, and can't dance. Elaine always has a witty comeback or a clever plan.

Cosmo Kramer, most often called Kramer, is Jerry's eccentric neighbor. He's known for his wild hair, quirks, and constantly bursting into Jerry's apartment without knocking. Kramer has no steady job and seems to float through life with confidence.

George Costanza is one of Jerry's best friends and is known for his balding head, glasses, and pessimistic outlook. He is insecure about his appearance, job prospects, and relationships. His parents, Frank and Estelle Costanza, are also significant characters in the show.
